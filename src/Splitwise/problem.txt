Design Splitwise.
Splitwise helps groups of people track shared expenses and settle debts easily. It's commonly used among friends,
roommates, coworkers, or anyone splitting costs for shared activities like trips, meals, or household expenses.

Requirements:
1. Register/login Users.
2. Users can add expenses.
3. Users can edit expenses.
4. Also, users can settle expenses.
5. Allow users to make groups and add, edit and settle expenses in the group.
6. Enable/disable simplified group expense feature.
